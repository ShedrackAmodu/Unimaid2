# Patron User Journey: From Research to Return

**Scenario: A student's complete library experience for a term paper project**

1. **Research Planning**
   - Student logs into their library account from home
   - Searches the catalog for books on their research topic
   - Adds promising titles to a personal reading list for later

2. **Resource Discovery and Reservation**
   - Browses detailed book information and availability
   - Places reservations on books that are currently checked out
   - Explores the institutional repository for related theses and papers
   - Downloads open-access articles to their device

3. **Digital Access Setup**
   - Starts a digital session for remote access to e-journals
   - Browses licensed databases for scholarly articles
   - Saves citations and creates notes on relevant findings

4. **Physical Borrowing**
   - Visits the library and checks notifications on their phone
   - Picks up reserved books from the hold shelf
   - Borrows additional books found during browsing
   - Books a study room for group work sessions

5. **Active Study Period**
   - Uses reserved study room for collaborative research
   - Renews books online when due dates approach
   - Receives automated reminders about upcoming due dates
   - Accesses digital resources during study breaks

6. **Event Participation**
   - Registers for a research workshop offered by the library
   - Attends the session and learns new research techniques
   - Networks with other students and library staff

7. **Fine Management**
   - Notices an overdue book and accruing fines
   - Pays the fine securely through the online portal
   - Receives confirmation and cleared account status

8. **Return and Reflection**
   - Returns all borrowed books at the circulation desk
   - Reviews their borrowing history and reading patterns
   - Provides feedback on books through the system
   - Plans future visits based on successful research experience

9. **Ongoing Engagement**
   - Stays subscribed to library newsletters
   - Continues to use digital resources for ongoing studies
   - Recommends the library to classmates
   - Maintains active membership for continued access
