# Staff User Journey: A Day in the Life of a Librarian

**Scenario: Managing daily library operations and assisting patrons**

1. **Morning Setup and System Check**
   - Librarian arrives and logs into the staff dashboard
   - Reviews overnight reservations and due dates
   - Checks equipment status and room bookings for the day

2. **Opening Circulation Desk**
   - Processes overnight returns and updates book conditions
   - Handles morning rush of patrons returning books
   - Assists students with account questions and password resets

3. **Managing Reservations and Loans**
   - Notifies patrons about available reserved books via email/SMS
   - Processes checkouts for reserved items
   - Helps patrons find books on shelves when locations are unclear

4. **Catalog Maintenance**
   - Adds new book acquisitions to the system
   - Updates book locations and conditions from recent inventory
   - Corrects metadata errors reported by patrons

5. **Patron Assistance and Problem Solving**
   - Resolves overdue notices and fine payment issues
   - Helps with interlibrary loan requests
   - Assists with research questions and database access

6. **Digital Services Oversight**
   - Monitors active digital sessions and resource usage
   - Helps patrons start e-library sessions for remote access
   - Troubleshoots technical issues with online databases

7. **Afternoon Workflow Management**
   - Processes bulk check-ins from study rooms
   - Manages room bookings and space conflicts
   - Prepares for evening events and workshops

8. **Event Support and Community Engagement**
   - Sets up registration for library workshops
   - Moderates comments on library blog posts
   - Assists with event check-in and attendance tracking

9. **End-of-Day Procedures**
   - Generates daily circulation reports
   - Updates patron records with special notes
   - Prepares holds and notices for next day's patrons

10. **Closing and Handover**
    - Ensures all books are properly shelved
    - Updates system status for night operations
    - Hands over outstanding issues to night staff
