    1. Acceptance of Terms

    By accessing and using the Ramat Library services, you accept and agree to be bound by the terms and provision of this agreement. If you do not agree to abide by the above, please do not use this service.

    2. Library Membership

    Eligibility
    • Current students, faculty, and staff of the University of Maiduguri
    • Alumni and community members may apply for external membership
    • Valid university identification is required for membership
    Responsibilities
    • Maintain current contact information
    • Report lost or stolen library materials immediately
    • Return borrowed materials by the due date
    • Respect library policies and other users

    3. Borrowing Policies

    Loan Periods
    • Books: 14 days for undergraduates, 28 days for postgraduates and faculty
    • Journals and magazines: Library use only
    • Reference materials: Library use only
    • Reserve materials: Limited to 2 hours
    Renewals
    • Books may be renewed up to 3 times if no holds are placed
    • Renewals may be done online or in person
    • Overdue materials cannot be renewed

    4. Computer and Internet Use

    Library computers and internet access are provided for academic and research purposes. Users agree to:

    • Use computers for legal and ethical purposes only
    • Not download copyrighted materials illegally
    • Not engage in harassment or inappropriate behavior
    • Respect time limits when others are waiting
    • Not modify computer settings or install software

    5. Study Room Usage

    • Study rooms are available on a first-come, first-served basis
    • Maximum usage time is 4 hours per day
    • Rooms must be left clean and orderly
    • Food and drinks are not permitted
    • Noise levels must be kept appropriate for study

    6. Code of Conduct

    Users are expected to maintain a respectful and appropriate environment:

    • Treat library staff and other users with courtesy
    • Keep noise levels low and conversations brief
    • Respect library property and materials
    • Follow all university policies and local laws
    • Report any suspicious or inappropriate behavior

    7. Privacy and Confidentiality

    The library respects user privacy and maintains confidentiality of:

    • Borrowing records and reading history
    • Personal information provided to the library
    • Research and study habits

    However, library records may be disclosed when required by law or university policy.

    8. Digital Resources

    • Access to digital resources is limited to current library members
    • Sharing of login credentials is prohibited
    • Downloading of materials must comply with copyright laws
    • Commercial use of library resources is not permitted

    9. Termination of Access

    The library reserves the right to suspend or terminate library privileges for:

    • Violation of library policies
    • Outstanding fines or overdue materials
    • Abuse of library resources or facilities
    • Illegal activities conducted on library premises
