Access freely available scholarly materials and research outputs without subscription barriers. Our open access collection promotes knowledge sharing and academic collaboration.

What is Open Access?

Open Access (OA) refers to the practice of providing unrestricted access to research outputs, allowing anyone to read, download, copy, distribute, print, search, or link to the full texts of scholarly articles without financial, legal, or technical barriers.

Free and Open

All resources in this collection are freely available to read, download, and share without requiring a subscription or payment.

How to Find Open Access Materials

Search Strategies
  • Use #OpenAccess in searches
  • Check publisher websites for OA options
  • Use OA-specific databases and repositories
  • Look for Creative Commons licenses
License Information

Understanding Creative Commons licenses:

  • CC BY: Attribution required
  • CC BY-SA: Share alike required
  • CC BY-NC: Non-commercial use only
  • CC0: Public domain dedication

Publishing Open Access

Want to Publish Open Access?

Consider these options for making your research openly available:

  • Gold OA: Publish in open access journals
  • Green OA: Self-archive in repositories
  • Hybrid OA: Pay to make articles open in subscription journals
  • Platinum OA: No fees for authors or readers
