What are Open Educational Resources?

Open Educational Resources (OER) are teaching, learning, and research materials that are either in the public domain or have been released under an open license that permits free use, adaptation, and distribution.

5R Permissions

OER materials allow you to: Retain - make and keep copies, Reuse - use in various ways, Revise - adapt and modify, Remix - combine with other materials, Redistribute - share with others.

Finding OER Materials

Search Strategies
  • Use subject-specific keywords
  • Filter by license type
  • Look for peer-reviewed content
  • Check file formats and compatibility
License Verification

Always check the license terms:

  • CC BY: Attribution required
  • CC BY-SA: Share alike required
  • CC BY-NC: Non-commercial use
  • Public Domain: No restrictions
