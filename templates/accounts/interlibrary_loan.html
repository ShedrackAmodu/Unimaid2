Interlibrary Loan Services

Access materials from libraries around the world through our interlibrary loan service. We can help you obtain books, articles, and other resources not available in our collection.

What is Interlibrary Loan?

Interlibrary Loan (ILL) is a service that allows you to borrow materials from other libraries when they are not available in our collection. This service helps expand your access to research materials and academic resources worldwide.

Global Network Access

Through our partnerships with libraries worldwide, you can access millions of additional resources beyond our local collection.

Types of Materials Available

Books & Monographs

Full-length books and monographs from partner libraries.

  • Loan periods vary by lending library
  • Delivery typically 3-7 business days
  • Physical books shipped to our library
  • Photocopy options available
Journal Articles

Individual articles from academic journals and periodicals.

  • Electronic delivery preferred
  • Usually available within 1-3 days
  • PDF format for easy access
  • Copyright compliance maintained
Theses & Dissertations

Academic theses and dissertations from other institutions.

  • Access to research from universities worldwide
  • Digital copies typically provided
  • Comprehensive search assistance available
  • Graduate-level research support
Special Materials

Conference proceedings, technical reports, and other specialized materials.

  • Statistical reports and data
  • Audiovisual materials (limited availability)
  • Maps and government documents
  • Archival materials (case by case)

How to Request Materials

Step 1: Check Our Collection

Before requesting, verify the item is not available in our library.

  • Search our online catalog
  • Check electronic databases
  • Ask a librarian for assistance
  • Verify complete citation information
Step 2: Submit Request

Complete the interlibrary loan request form with all required information.

  • Your library account information
  • Complete bibliographic details
  • Desired delivery date (if applicable)
  • Contact information
Step 3: Wait for Processing

Our staff will process your request and contact partner libraries.

  • We search our network of partner libraries
  • We contact multiple libraries if needed
  • We follow up to ensure timely delivery
  • You'll be notified when materials arrive
Step 4: Receive & Use Materials

Pick up physical materials or access electronic copies.

  • Physical items picked up at service desk
  • Electronic items accessed via email link
  • Observe loan periods and return dates
  • Follow all lending library requirements
